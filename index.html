A fun fact about your name🌊🌊

Did you know that your name is historically very significant? The ancient Indians used to call the river we know as Indus as Sindhu . The Persians, living on the other side of the river, called it Hindu, as they had difficulties pronouncing 'S'. The Greeks, who came after the Persians, called it Indus, from which comes India.
